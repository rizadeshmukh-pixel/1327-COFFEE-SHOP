Our Story

Not just a café — a refuge

Tucked inside Rustomjee Global City, Virar West, 1327 Coffee Shop was born from a simple belief: exceptional coffee shouldn't cost a fortune. Founded by Shubham and Jash, this intimate sanctuary is where Virar comes to breathe, work, and connect.

From meticulously pulled espressos to the legendary Vietnamese Iced Coffee and that world-class brownie — every item on our curated menu is crafted with love, precision, and an obsession for quality that punches far above its price point.
